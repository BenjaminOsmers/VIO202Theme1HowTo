How to Make Homemade Pizza

This is a how-to guide

1

Let's Kick Things of With Something Simple

  • Start by switching on the oven and preheating it to 230°C.

  • Switch on the kettle and boil roughly a cup of water.

  • Get your wine glass closer and fill it up.

2

This One is a Piece of Cake

  • Pour one cup of the water that you just boiled, into a mixing bowl.

  • Add a pinch of sugar.

  • Empty one packet of dry yeast into the bowl.

  • Stir until both the yeast and the sugar has dissolved.

  • Let it stand for about 10 minutes, or until creamy.

  • Give your glass of wine some attention while you wait.

3

Time to Get Dirty

  • Add 30ml of olive oil ot the mixture along with a pinch of salt.

  • Stir in 2 ½ cups of bread flour, beat the dough until smooth.

4

Did Somebody Say Powertools?

  • Transfer the dough from the mixing bowl to a countertop mixer. Set the mixer to low speed and let it do it’s thing. This will help smoothen out your dough even more.

5

Time for More Manual Labour
but first... a sip of wine

  • Remove the dough from the mixer and place it in a lightly oiled bowl.

  • Let it rest for about 10 minutes.

  • You know what to do in the mean time...

  • Next up, turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and start rolling it, your aim is to end up with a circular piece of dough roughly the size and thickness of a pizza base.

6

Enjoying Yourself? Wait Till You See What's Next In Line!

  • Firstly, place your circular piece of dough on a pizza stone or on pan. Spread tomato paste over the whole piece of dough. Now it's time to add the toppings!
